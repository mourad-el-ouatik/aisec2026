What is Capture the Flag?

A Capture The Flag (CTF) competition is a cybersecurity challenge that tests participants' hacking skills. The goal is to find hidden flags within a set time frame. Flags are usually hidden in vulnerable systems, applications, or networks. Participants must exploit vulnerabilities and extract the flags.


About AISEC CTF

This event is a cybersecurity competition organized by CLUB GCDSTE at ENSA Marrakech as part of the AISEC event — the International Summit on Artificial Intelligence & Cybersecurity.
