INSTRUCTIONS

overview

GENERAL OVERVIEW

Format

Online competition with an onsite option exclusively for students from Marrakech. Participate from anywhere in the world.

Eligibility

Open to all cybersecurity students. The finals are exclusively for students, verified through official student attestations.

Duration

12 hours of non-stop hacking — from 9:00 PM to 9:00 AM. The clock never stops. Neither should you.

Challenge Type — Jeopardy Style

Participants will face challenges across various cybersecurity categories. Solve challenges, accumulate points, and climb the scoreboard.

  • Web Exploitation
  • Cryptography
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Forensics
  • Binary Exploitation
  • Miscellaneous
  • OSINT
phases

COMPETITION PHASES

Phase 1 — Qualification

APRIL 15–16, 2025

  • Format: Online & Onsite (Marrakech students)
  • Eligibility: Open to all cybersecurity students
  • Duration: 12 hours (9pm → 9am)
  • Type: Jeopardy Style challenges
  • Selected teams advance to the Finals
  • This phase is exclusively for students, verified through student attestations

Phase 2 — Finals

MAY 13–14, 2025 — ONSITE

  • Jeopardy Round: All finalist teams compete
  • Attack-Defense Round: Exclusively for the TOP 10 ranked in the Jeopardy Round
  • Finals are held onsite at ENSA Marrakech
  • Winners announced at the AISEC 2025 ceremony on May 14th

TEAM REGISTRATION

Team Size

Teams must consist of 3 to 5 members. Solo participation is not allowed. Assemble your crew and hack together.

Team Name

Each team must register with a unique team name. Choose wisely — your reputation starts here.

Registration Process

Only the team captain must fill out the registration form. Provide accurate member information for verification.
