Overview

About AiSec 2024

A three-day programme spanning keynotes, workshops, peer-reviewed research sessions, and a live on-site CTF competition at ENSA Marrakech.

The 2024 edition marked a turning point for AiSec, expanding beyond its academic origins into a nationally recognised conference with representation from industry leaders, government cybersecurity bodies, and top research institutions across Morocco and the MENA region.

For the first time, the programme included a dedicated industry track where practitioners presented real-world deployments of AI-driven security tooling — from anomaly detection in SOC environments to automated incident-response pipelines.

The research track accepted 14 peer-reviewed papers, with best paper honours awarded in both the Applied Security and Theoretical AI categories. All accepted papers were compiled into the AiSec 2024 proceedings.

CTF Finals 2024

The 2024 CTF was the largest edition yet, drawing 20 teams through an online qualification round before the top 8 competed live at ENSA Marrakech.

Challenges spanned web exploitation, cryptography, digital forensics, binary analysis, and a novel Adversarial AI category — where teams attacked and defended deployed machine learning models in real-time.

The final scoreboard was contested down to the last hour, with the winning team solving the AI challenge that stumped most competitors.
