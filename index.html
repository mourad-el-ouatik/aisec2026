About the Event

What is AiSec?

▶
Event Teaser Soon ...
AISEC 2026 — Official Teaser Video

AiSec is the leading international summit dedicated to Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity, held annually at ENSA National School of Applied Science, Cadi Ayad University, Marrakech, Morocco.

The summit brings together world-class researchers, industry leaders, government officials, and students from Africa, the Arab World, and beyond to explore the intersection of AI and cyber threats.

AiSec features a Scientific Conference with peer-reviewed papers, an international CTF Competition, expert keynotes, hands-on workshops, and high-level networking sessions.
