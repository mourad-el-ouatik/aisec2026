Submission Guidelines

  • Contributions must describe original, state-of-the-art unpublished research, not currently under review by another conference or journal.

  • For the PhD best dissertation award, the doctoral candidate must submit a one-page nomination letter signed by their Supervisor/Department or Institute Head.
  • Submissions via SciencesConf: aisec25.sciencesconf.org
