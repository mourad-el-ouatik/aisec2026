About the Event

Welcome to AISEC 2026

Attracting a global audience, the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity (AISEC 2026) is a premier event in the fields of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and sustainable technologies.

It aims to showcase cutting-edge research and innovative solutions in AI-driven cybersecurity, addressing the challenges and opportunities in securing digital systems.

The AISEC 2026 will be held at the National School of Applied Sciences (ENSA), Marrakech, Morocco, on May 16–18, 2026, in collaboration with leading research institutions and industry partners.
